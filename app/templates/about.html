About SprintIQ

Redefining Agile velocity with the precision of Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

To eliminate the uncertainty in software planning. We bridge the gap between human intuition and data driven reality, enabling engineering teams to deliver on time, every time.

The Origin

SprintIQ was born from a frustration common to every developer: Estimation Poker. What should be a quick sync often turns into a 30-minute debate over abstract numbers.

We realized that historical data holds the answer. By training models on thousands of completed user stories, we built a system that offers an objective baseline—freeing teams to focus on the "how" rather than the "how long".

The Vision

We envision a future where Sprint Planning is automated. Where AI suggests the points, identifies risks before code is written, and helps product managers write clearer requirements.

SprintIQ is the first step towards that future—a smart companion for the modern Agile team.
