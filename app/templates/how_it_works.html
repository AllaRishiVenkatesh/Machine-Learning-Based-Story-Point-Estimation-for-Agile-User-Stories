How It Works

From raw text to actionable insights in milliseconds.

01

Enter User Story

Paste your standard format user story: "As a [role], I want [feature], so that [benefit]".

02

Preprocessing & Extraction

The system cleans the text, removes stop words, and vectorizes the semantic meaning (TF-IDF/Embeddings).

03

Model Inference

Our pre-trained Random Forest Regressor calculates the complexity score based on feature vectors.

04

Prediction & Confidence

The raw score is mapped to the nearest Fibonacci point, and a confidence score is assigned.

Human in Control

SprintIQ is a decision-support tool. Your team always has the final say. We just provide the data-driven baseline.
